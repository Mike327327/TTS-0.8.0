TTS Personalization

This page shows results of the models trained for a Czech TTS system.
First, results of base models are presented along with examples.
This is followed by example of non-fine-tuned and fine-tuned samples (male and female speakers).
Each section is briefly introduced and the results are commented.

Base models

1M and 200k

The 1M model is capable of generating shorter samples conditioned on d-vectors in a decent quality (samples B1 and B2). However, longer inputs caused issues and speech was unintelligible (sample 3). This issues was solved by segmenting the input text based on commas (sample 4). The 200k model performed worse than the 1M model. Segmenting the input text sometimes did not help and speech was not intelligible, as can heard in sample 5.
